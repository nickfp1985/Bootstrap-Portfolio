About Me

My name is Nick Peddie. I have been a poorly self-taught web developer for the last two years, as a hobby. I started officially programming with Java around five years ago to become a software engineer. Some odd things happened with the school I was attending and I decided to try my luck elsewhere. Since I made that move, I have been teaching myself html, css, and javascript with the help of online programming courses from a number of different websites; Udacity, Udemy, EdX, and Coursera to name a few.

I am now attending UCSD's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp in hopes that their career services will teach me how to package myself as the prime candidate for any Full Stack Web Developer position that I know I am. I am very dedicated to become fluent in everything web development to change my career. I have a good job now, but I want to have a job in something that I enjoy every day and I that can be achieved with being a Full Stack Developer.

I am excited about the future and the new opportunities that this UCSD program will open up to me. What I lack in knowledge I make up for with my drive to learn and master new technologies, tools, and programming languages.
It certainly helps that I have close friends who have been developers for years that I can call on whenever I need them. Let's do this!
